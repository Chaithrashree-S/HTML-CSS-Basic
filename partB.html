DOG BREEDS

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds in the United States. They are friendly, intelligent, and devoted dogs. They are well-suited for families and are known for their gentle temperament and willingness to please. Golden Retrievers are often used as service dogs, therapy dogs, and search and rescue dogs due to their versatility and trainability.

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is another highly popular dog breed known for its friendly and outgoing nature. Labs are intelligent, loyal, and versatile, making them excellent family pets, working dogs, and service animals. They are often used as guide dogs for the blind, therapy dogs, and search and rescue dogs due to their gentle temperament and high trainability.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a breed known for its intelligence, strength, and versatility. They are often used as police dogs, search and rescue dogs, and service animals due to their obedience and ability to learn complex tasks. German Shepherds are loyal and protective of their families, making them excellent guard dogs as well.
